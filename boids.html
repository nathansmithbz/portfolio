Boids

An example of Boids written in the JavaScript library p5.js.

A simple set of rules can create complex flocking behaviour as seen in birds.

  • separation: steer to avoid crowding local flockmates.
  • alignment: steer towards the average heading of local flockmates
  • cohesion: steer to move towards the average position (center of mass) of local flockmates
Controls
  • Use the + and - keys to add or remove predators.
  • Use the > and < keys to add or remove boids.
  • P Shows the lines of influence for the chasing predator
  • B Shows the lines of influence for the flocking boids
